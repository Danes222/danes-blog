Hello World!, Danes Artworks is a personal blog project by me, Danes Nizam Virnanda.

The reason I created this blog is to socialize and to form a digital artist community, where this is very difficult to do, because in the world of digital art there are various branches of art, and each of them has its own market. So what you waiting on? Let's explore, because that is what artists do!
